About us

We are a group of Dynamics NAV, 365 Business Central, GP and Salesforce CRM developers and consultants.

Originally, we started implementing Dynamics NAV (also known as "Navision") only. That's how the name of the company arose. Despite of the fact, that NAV and Business Central are our main focus, throughout the years we started implementing, supporting and customizing other products, including Microsoft Dynamics GP and Salesforce CRM. We also do have robust practical experience when it comes to the integration of these products with each other and third party systems.

If your company wants to implement Dynamics or Salesforce products, or just switch your unreliable Microsoft Dynamics or Salesforce partner - we will be very happy to assist you.
